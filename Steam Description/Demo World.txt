Demonstration world for ARMS

Requires [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=363880940] ARMS [/url]

All of the Autopilot blocks are initially turned off.
Where the "Commands" group exists, the Autopilot will be reading commands from that text panel.

Drones:
Asteroid mining of Uranium
Automatic Scout, using radar and laser antenna
Fighting Drone
Missile

Other:
Antenna Relay & Radar with Display
Solar arrays that track the sun
Weapon Control including rotor-turret
